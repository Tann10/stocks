Data Source


  • Health data: The data is collected from the World Bank Repository. Original source is the World Health Organization Global Health Expenditure Database. The current health expenditure (% of GDP) does not include capital health expenditures such as buildings, machinery, IT, and stocks of vaccines for emergencies or outbreaks. Data is not available for European Countries. For more information click here

  • Education data: The World Bank EdStats Query holds around 2,500 internationally comparable education indicators for access, progression, completion, literacy, teachers, population, and expenditures. The indicators cover the education cycle from pre-primary to tertiary education. Data is not available for China, France, Saudi Arabia, and Turkey. For more information click here

  • Military data: The data is collected from World Bank Repository. Military data is collected from Stockholm Internation Peace Research Institute SIPRI derived from Nato which includes current and capital expenditure on armed forces, defense ministries and other government agencies engaged in defense projects. Data is available for all G20 countries. For more information click here
