Standard & Poor's (SnP) 500 Companies Stock Analysis

The S&P 500 Index or the Standard & Poor's 500 Index is a market-capitalization-weighted index of 500 of the largest publicly traded companies in the U.S.. It is not an exact list of the top 500 U.S. companies by market capitalization. because there are other criteria to be included in the index. The S&P is a float-weighted index, meaning company market capitalizations are adjusted by the number of shares available for public trading.
The main data source for this website is Yahoo Finance
The purpose of this website is to help students, analysts, trader or anyone who is in search of stocks information to visualize the data. Although, I have mentioned about trading strategies, I am not recommending any investment/trading decisions and one must do a thorough research before investing. The visualization created is very informative, easy to use, and helpful to derive interesting insights. Feel free to explore further by clicking on the sections below or using navigation links.
