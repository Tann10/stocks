G20 Countries Expenditure across 6 years

  1. As a % of GDP, Brazil has been consistently spending higher followed by South Africa, Argentina and United Kingdom. Whereas per capita expenditure is highest in the USA followed by Australia and Canada
  2. Education Expenditure data is missing for China, France, Saudi Arabia, Turkey
  3. Japan has lowest education expenditure as a percentage of GDP whereas India is lowest in terms of per capita

G20 Countries Education Expenditure Percentage Change

  1. In terms of % of GDP, Indonesia has highest jump from 2014-2015 (8.99%) whereas Mexico has highest decline in expenditure in from 2016
  2. In terms of per capita, Russian shows a lot of fluctuation such as there was drop by 36% in year 2015 and increase by 23% in 2017
  3. India, Indonesia, and USA shows increase in per capita education expenditure every year

G20 Countries GDP per Capita versus Expenditure per Capita
