Welcome to G20 Health Expenditure Analysis

G20 Countries Expenditure across 6 years

  1. As a % of GDP, the USA has been consistently spending more followed by France, Germany, and Japan. Even per capita expenditure is highest in the USA.
  2. Health Expenditure data is missing for European Union
  3. India has lowest health expenditure per capita and as a percentage of GDP

G20 Countries Health Expenditure Percentage Change

  1. In terms of % of GDP, Argentina, and Saudi Arabia showed sudden changes in health expenditure from year 2015 to 2017 whereas Brazil shows a linear decline
  2. In terms of fixed value dollar expenditure, Argentina and Russia have the largest deviations. A similar pattern is observed in terms of per capita expenditure
  3. Surprisingly, France, Germany, and Italy show a similar pattern in terms of percentage change of per capita expenditure

G20 Countries GDP per Capita versus Expenditure per Capita

  1. Graph shows that there is a positive linear relationship between GDP per capita and Expenditure per capita
  2. Countries having high population have lesser expenditure per capita
