Welcome to G20 Health Expenditure Analysis

G20 Countries Expenditure across 6 years

  1. As a % of GDP, USA has been consistently spending higher followed by France, Germany and Japan. Even Per Capita expenditure is highest in the USA.
  2. Health Expenditure data is missing for European Union
  3. India has lowest health expenditure per capita and as a percentage of GDP

G20 Countries Health Expenditure Percentage Change

  1. In terms of % of GDP, Argentia and Saudi Arabia showed sudden changes in health expenditure from year 2015 to 2017 whereas Brazil shows a linear decline
  2. In terms of fixed value dollar expenditure, Argentia and Russian Federation has largest deviations, similar pattern is observed in terms of per capita expenditure
  3. Surprisingly, France, Germnay and Italy show similar pattern in percentage change of per capita expenditure

G20 Countries GDP per Capita verses Expenditure per Capita

  1. Graph shows there is positive linear relationship between GDP per capita and Expenditure per capita
  2. Countries with very high population have lesser expenditure per capita
